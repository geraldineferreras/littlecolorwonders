# Image Assets Directory

This directory should contain your product images and visual assets.

## Required Images:

### Hero Section:
- `hero-banner.png` - Main product bundle preview (recommended: 800x600px)
- `logo.png` - Your business logo (recommended: 200x80px)
- `favicon.ico` - Website favicon (16x16, 32x32px)

### Product Mockups:
- `product-mockup1.png` - Featured product preview (400x300px)

- `space-explorer.png` - Space Explorer Pack preview
- `ocean-friends.png` - Ocean Friends Pack preview
- `farm-friends.png` - Farm Friends Pack preview
- `parent-planner.png` - Parent Planner preview

### Thumbnails:
- `thumb-coloring.png` - Coloring activity thumbnail (60x60px)
- `thumb-alphabet.png` - Alphabet activity thumbnail (60x60px)
- `thumb-maze.png` - Maze activity thumbnail (60x60px)
- `thumb-animals.png` - Animal activity thumbnail (60x60px)

## Image Specifications:
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Quality: High resolution for crisp printing
- Optimization: Compress for web to maintain fast loading
- Style: Bright, colorful, child-friendly designs
- Background: Transparent PNGs where applicable

## Tips:
1. Use consistent styling across all product images
2. Include actual samples of your printable content
3. Make images appealing to your target audience (parents/teachers)
4. Consider creating mockups showing printed pages in use
5. Optimize file sizes for web performance

Replace this file with your actual images when ready to launch!
